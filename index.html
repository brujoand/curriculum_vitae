Anders Brujordet

Lead Platform Engineer

I started out as a Java consultant focusing on Clean Code and agile methodologies, but I quickly got fascinated by the power and elegance of functional programming with Scala. This lead me to Finn where I got to learn more from a team of excelent scala developers. At Finn I also tried to improve the CI/CI platform and in doing so I got noticed by Schibsted, Finn's parent company. I was hired to be part of the Delivery team in Schibsted to provide a delivery Platform for 1500 developers. When the door to "DevOps" was finally opened many of it's areas caught my interest, and I later joined VPS to help them create a Delivery Platform from scratch while also focusing on improving security.

At home I'm a dad, husband, power lifter, guitarist and hobby tinkerer. The latter might by why DevOps and security resonate so well with me, as I love to take things a part and create new. I've created my own MagicMirror which displays among other things the next train to leave from my local station. I've built, soldered and programmed my own silenced mechanical keyboard which I use every day both at home and at work and when kubernetes was made available I was quick to create my own cluster at home using Raspberry pis and Lego. All my computers and network utilities report metrics and logs using Prometheus, Grafana and Graylog.
